How RunRelease Works

  1. Set Your Schedule: Choose your running distance, time window, and active days.
  2. Select Apps to Lock: Pick which apps you want to keep locked until you complete your run.
  3. Start Your Run: Begin your GPS-tracked run directly from the app.
  4. Track Your Progress: Watch your distance and route update in real time.
  5. Unlock Apps: Once you reach your goal, your selected apps unlock automatically.
  6. Emergency Access: Need urgent access? Use the emergency unlock for safety.
