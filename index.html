How to Use RunRelease

  1. Select apps you want to lock until you finish your run.
  2. Set your daily running goal (distance or destination).
  3. Start your run and let RunRelease track your progress via GPS.
  4. Stay motivated—locked apps remain inaccessible until you complete your goal.
  5. Finish your run to instantly unlock your selected apps.
